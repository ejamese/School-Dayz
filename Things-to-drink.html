Things to drink

Sunday

Katzen Jammers

Get the Oktoberfest feeling all year round with an authentic Bierhall experience. Located at Borough Market, KatzenJammers offers a modern take on a traditional German Bierhall, mixed with craft beers and delicious brews. Enjoy a Bavarian menu and a pint of the good stuff until 11pm Sunday to Monday. Oom pah pah!

Monday

Experimental Cocktail Club

Sometimes the best places are behind battered doors you wouldn't expect, and that's certainly the case with the Experimental Cocktail Club hidden in the heart of Soho behind an unassuming door. Spread over three floors, devour the taste of London's best cocktails created by the best mixologists in London, with a charming Parisian flair taken from the 1920's speakeasies. Tuesday

Blitzen

If the hustle and bustle of the big city bars don't tempt you, then Blixen is the place to retreat to for some laid back mid-week drinks. Located in an old bank, Blixen is a European brassiere with menus to suit breakfast, lunch , dinner, snacks and more, with a laid back feel that will definitely brighten up your Wednesday Instagram feed. Be sure to book to avoid disappointment, it can get pretty packed up in that bunker.
