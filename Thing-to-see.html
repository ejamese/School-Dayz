Things to see

Sunday

Skating at Somerset House

If you want ot embrace your inner Christmas rom-com character, then skating at the iconic Somerset House is for you. Sponsered every year by the British institution that is Fortnum and Mason, you can expect beautiful scenes and glorious lighting as you and you friends skate in the crisp Winter air. A brilliant first date choice, your Tinder schedule will fill up immensley as you will want to go back again and again. Wrap up warm and wear big thick socks - because there were no blisters in Love Actually.

Location 1

Monday

Carol Concert at Southwark Cathedral

Even though singing Christmas Carols may bring back awkward primary school memories, we would definitely recommend popping along to this incredible concert at the awe-inspiring Southwark Cathedral. The Cathedral boasts two chamber choirs belting out the carols of your childhood, and even incorporating some classic Christmas hits in there to keep your foot tapping. A wondrous night that will reinforce the Christmas spirit in you. There won't be a dry eye in the house once 'O Little Town of Bethlehem' gets going, we can promise you that.

Location 2

Tuesday

Carnaby Street Lights

Forget the hustle and bustle of Oxford Street lights, and lose yourself in the back streets of Carnaby, tucked away behind Liberty London and providing the inspiration this Christmas. With messages of Love, Hope, and Peace, the Carnaby lights 2016 are definitely needed after, well, a pretty rocky year. As they beam down on your, take your Instagram and explore the bars and shops that adorn the area - pop into The Diner for a Juicy Lucy burger, or for some cocktails in one of the renowned cocktail bars. Christmas can be different this year, we'll meet you there.
